Which homestay should you stay at this season in Dalat to watch the rain ‘how much’?

April 26, 2022 by Thien Di

Just having the ultimate rain-watching view, and also the “billion-dollar” location of the hit MV series, these 4 Dalat homestays below will not make you admire.

Da Lat is already in the rainy season, what if you get lost in this city but can’t go anywhere because “rain is everywhere”?

Are you bored with hundreds of photo styles that are damaged everywhere?

Don’t worry, the following 4 Dalat homestay suggestions will be an interesting check-in place to dispel the boredom called “rainy season tourism”. With large windows, opening a poetic and romantic natural scene, watching the rain also becomes “chilling”, maybe it will make you “addicted to the rain” but lying in a warm blanket and watching the rain fly. flutter.

1. Jang and Min’s House

If you are a fan of the MV series “Flower bloom – Flower bloom” by female singer Nguyen Ha, you will certainly not be able to ignore Jang & Min’s House.

Jang and Min owns rooms with large windows, opening an airy space. Photo: @tondidau.

This is a romantic and gentle homestay located on Khe Sanh street, only about 4 kilometers from the city center. Not noisy, bustling, Jang and Min’s House has a poetic beauty like a classic European-style wooden house.

It’s raining, lying curled up in a blanket, enjoying a cup of tea and watching the water droplets on the porch is a feeling that can’t be better. Photo: @quan1708.

This place has the architecture like a European-style garden house. Photo: @imdew211.

With lush green gardens and large arched windows, Jang and Min looks so romantic and warm, you can watch the rain for hours, sip a hot cup of coffee and enjoy the melodies of nature. – a feeling could not be more “Da Lat”.

Jang ang Min’s kitchen is the dream corner of the sister association “Love the kitchen”. Photo: @embematngu.

A tea corner with a view that could not be better. Photo: @milkseptember.

Address: 11bis Khe Sanh, Ward 10, City. Da Lat.

2. Nomini Homestay

No more smoke or noise with the aftertaste of the city, this homestay in Da Lat is just like a small house in the middle of a deserted town that Den Vau often dreams about in the song “Ten Years”.

Nomini Homestay looks like a Japanese garden house from the outside. Photo: @puravidahomes.

Nomini is like a peaceful corner to welcome the sound of pine trees. The transparent windows in gentle wood tones create a feeling of curling up in a blanket while still enjoying Dalat properly.

The feeling of curling up in a blanket with a green view is not found everywhere. Photo: @khongdisaoden.

If Jang and Min are peaceful and calm, Nomini brings a little more joy. The modern, new but still pure Asian architecture is the first feeling when we step into that space – an ethereal, gentle Japanese garden house style.

Nomini owns an airy garden with a romantic hill view. Photo: @nominihomestay.

This place holds the joys and romances only in Da Lat. Photo: @gisele.tran.

Address: 04 Nguyen Khuyen, Ward 5, City. Da Lat.

3. The Kupid – Da Lat no single

The Kupid – Dalat no Single owns a luxurious view like Europe. Photo: The KUPID – Dalat no single.

There is no doubt that The Kupid is the golden name in the village of “good view” when it comes to homestays in Da Lat. Only about 2 kilometers from Xuan Huong Lake, Kupid’s green space will make you enjoy an extremely luxurious Da Lat with large glass doors to see green pine trees or enjoy the feeling of taking a bath while enjoying the extreme nature. chill.

A romantic view of Kupid, with transparent glass, watching the rain or dawn pops up the very “Da Lat” atmosphere. Photo: The KUPID – Dalat no single.

Bathing while watching the pine hill is a great experience at The Kupid. Photo: The KUPID – Dalat no single

The Kupid has the appearance of a luxurious European-style Homestay. You can both curl up in a blanket watching the romantic view, and walk around the pine hill to enjoy the fresh air.

The scenic bathtub is the million-like photo corner at The Kupid. Photo: @dao.kyanh.

Address: 47 Dang Thai Than, Ward 3, City. Da Lat.

4. Firewood Homestay

Firewood Homestay – a house inspired by the countryside. Photo: @itsffox.

“What’s the name of the house” – Cui Homestay, just like the name, is a homestay with all lovely wooden houses, but the miracle of wood is nature, creating a warm feeling that is difficult to explain.

Firewood creates a feeling of warmth and peace. Photo: @mai_lan_huong_ly88.

Firewood overall looks like a garden house, creating a rural and peaceful feeling with luxuriant and luxuriant trees. In particular, with a poetic and dreamy attic floor, it promises to be a good view for believers who like to watch the rain or sunrise in Da Lat.

Firewood has very gentle green corners and is full of warm sunshine. Photo: Da Lat Cui Homestay.

Attic – a beautiful place to watch the rain or enjoy the sunrise. Photo: @hiensophie10.

Address: 79 Trieu Viet Vuong, Ward 3, City. Da Lat.

“If you want to have a new feeling, enjoy a more complete Da Lat from food, accommodation, to sleep, why not try these 4 Dalat homestays. Don’t be afraid of sunshine or rain, because no matter how humid the weather is, you can have a complete Da Lat.”
