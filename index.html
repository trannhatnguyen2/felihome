Our Events

Jan 02, 2019 Event
Vu Lan Festival

In Hoi An, this holiday is celebrated with plenty of activities turning off the lights and releasing the lanterns at 19:00. All create a brilliant and peaceful scene throughout the old town. Vu Lan ceremony has many meanings to help children show their filial piety and appreciate the gratitude of their parents and grandparents who were born and raised.

Apr 04, 2022 Event
Hot Air Balloon Festival

The balloons will fly freely at an altitude of 100-300 meters, within 5 km from the take-off point. In addition, hot air balloons are hanging at the height of 50m to serve tourists; and mini hot air balloons perform at Ky Dai yard (Hue Citadel) to help tourists visit and take photos.

May 2022 Event
Da Nang International Fireworks Festival (DIFF)

This large-scale event every year has a unique and new feature. With your expression, visitors to Da Nang will have the opportunity to admire the fantastic and elaborately invested fireworks performances. The fireworks jubilantly exploded in the sky of Da Nang, a brilliant space with impressive flowers, hearts, and stars.
